Spanish Moss

Scientific Name:

Tillandsia Usneoides

Fun Facts:

It is an epiphytic flowering plant that often grows on large trees in tropical and subtropical climates.

It is native to much of Mexico, Bermuda, the Bahamas, Central America, South America, the Southern United States, and West Indies.

It has been naturalized in Queensland, Australia, and is known as "grandpa's beard" in French Polynesia.
